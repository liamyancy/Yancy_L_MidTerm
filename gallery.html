Our Beer Gallery

Black + Decker Beer comes in four unique flavours for you to choose from. Our first and original Beer is the "Black + Decker Classic Lager" described as a beautiful amber lager with low butterscotch flavour, full bodied and a smooth post palate sensation.

You could also choose to indulge in one of our different flavours we have available. We have Natural Lemon, Natural Melon, and Natural Blueberry. All brewed with great tastes we're sure you would enjoy!
